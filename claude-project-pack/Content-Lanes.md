# Content Lanes

Use these lanes to keep the posting calendar varied without losing focus.

## 1. Weekly Cadence

Goal:
- keep PsiAN visible and active

Good inputs:
- recent site updates
- general mission language
- a short point from a publication or blog post

## 2. Therapy Basics

Goal:
- explain why depth-oriented therapy matters

Good inputs:
- The Case for Depth Therapy
- Publications
- Therapy That Sticks

## 3. Research And Evidence

Goal:
- translate a study or statistic into a readable social post

Good inputs:
- Library
- Publications
- blog research pieces

## 4. AI And Privacy

Goal:
- show why PsiAN is concerned about AI substitutes, apps, and privacy

Good inputs:
- Tech
- PsiAN's position on mental health apps
- Advocacy

## 5. Insurance And Access

Goal:
- explain barriers to care in plain language

Good inputs:
- Insurance guide
- Advocacy
- Private Practice Hub

## 6. Member Spotlight

Goal:
- highlight people and work that embody PsiAN's mission

Good inputs:
- Blog
- Media
- Publications

## Simple Rule

If a topic lane is hard to write, ask:
- what is PsiAN trying to protect?
- what is the reader supposed to understand?
- what is the cleanest hook?
