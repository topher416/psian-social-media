# PsiAN Project Brief

## What PsiAN Is

Psychotherapy Action Network, or PsiAN, is a national advocacy organization focused on psychotherapy access and on defending the clinicians and clinical standards that make meaningful therapy possible.

The organization's public framing is consistent:
- Psychotherapy is human care
- Therapy of depth, insight, and relationship matters
- Automated substitutes and profit-driven shortcuts can undermine care
- Access, clinical standards, insurance, and privacy are recurring concerns

## What This Project Is For

This project helps create PsiAN social posts that:
- keep the organization's channels active every week
- turn existing PsiAN writing into social-ready content
- adapt the same idea for multiple platforms
- make the work easier for someone who has not managed a social account before

## The Job

Cassie is not expected to invent the organization's message from scratch.
She is expected to:
- read the current PsiAN site and related materials
- pick a topic lane
- identify the strongest point
- write a draft with a hook
- shape the same idea for each platform

## The Core Objective

Keep PsiAN visible with multiple posts per week.

Use:
- PsiAN publications
- PsiAN positions
- blog posts
- advocacy pages
- site language that explains the mission clearly

## Editorial Direction

The content should usually do three things:
1. State the issue clearly
2. Explain why it matters to therapy, clinicians, or patients
3. End with a simple next step, call to action, or pointer to PsiAN

## What Not To Do

- Do not sound like a marketing team selling a product
- Do not use fear as the main device
- Do not write walls of text when a post can be broken into a hook and a few supporting lines
- Do not rely on generic social copy that could belong to any nonprofit
- Do not ignore PsiAN's actual site language and positions

## If You Need A Simple Mental Model

Think of the work like this:
- PsiAN already has the research and stance
- Claude helps turn that into a hook
- Cassie turns that into a post
- the app and this doc kit keep the process organized
